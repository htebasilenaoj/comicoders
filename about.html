About Cambridge Fringe Festival

The Cambridge Fringe Festival is a community-oriented comedy festival, focusing on stand-up and improv.

We're a free, not-for-profit festival, aiming to promote and celebrate comedy in Cambridgeshire and beyond.

Previous Cambridge Fringe Festivals

Cambridge Fringe Festival has had two successful festivals, with the debut in 2018.

In 2019 the festival showcased 46 shows back to back across four venues: The Portland Arms, The Boathouse,
The Waterman and Thirsty.

The Organisers

The Cambridge Fringe was set up by two local comedians, Ali Warwood and Dan Farmer.
Ali and Dan have experience on the stage and organising their own comedy nights in the Cambridge area.
